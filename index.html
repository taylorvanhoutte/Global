Hey, y'all!

I want to take a bit of time to update you on my life. I just completed my first semester of college at Illinois State University working towards my bachelor's in Deaf and Hard of Hearing Education along with a minor in Learning Behavioral Sciences. I have truly loved everything about ISU.

I have been participating in RSLA (a sign language club), and Salt Company (an on-campus ministry). I choose to spend my free time with friends or in my Bible, strengthening my relationship with Jesus and I'm so proud to say that I recently have been baptized. I have loved this first semester and can't imagine my life at any other school.

WHY I'M GOING OVERSEARS?

Seeing all the beautiful ways God has influenced my life this semester, I can't wait to see all that He can do for me in the future. More importantly, I can't wait to see what He does with those around me.

My local church here at ISU believes that college students have the power to change the world. This summer, me and a team of four other students from Salt Company will be sent to a college campus in Central Africa to share the word of Jesus Christ.

The Bible calls us to not just evangelize to those around us but to go to the ends of the earth. I have been blessed with the opportunity to obey His word in such a special and life-altering way. This time will be spent depending on my relationship with Christ and helping others do the same.
